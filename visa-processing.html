FAQ

Common Visa Questions

How long does visa processing take?

Processing times vary by country. Kenya and Uganda take 3-5 days, Tanzania 5-7 days, East Africa 4-7 days, and Morocco 10-15 days.

What documents do I need?

Typically: valid passport, passport photos, application form, proof of funds, and travel itinerary. Requirements vary by country.

Can you guarantee visa approval?

While we cannot guarantee approval, our 98% success rate speaks to our expertise in preparing strong applications.

Do you offer express processing?

Yes, express processing is available for select countries at an additional fee. Contact us for details.

How do I track my application?

We provide real-time updates via WhatsApp and email throughout the processing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash payments at our Abuja office.
